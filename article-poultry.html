Poultry Farming: Modern Approaches for Profitability & Sustainability

Explore proven strategies in poultry production, disease management, feed efficiency, and market access.

Introduction

Poultry farming is one of the fastest-growing agribusiness sectors worldwide. At Grefana Ltd, we provide farmers with tailored solutions to maximize productivity, ensure animal welfare, and connect to profitable markets.

Key Areas of Focus

  • Breeds & Genetics: Selecting high-yield breeds for eggs and meat.
  • Housing Systems: Designing cost-effective, well-ventilated poultry houses.
  • Feed Management: Balanced diets with local feed formulations to reduce costs.
  • Health & Biosecurity: Vaccination programs and best practices to prevent diseases.
  • Market Linkages: Helping farmers access buyers, processors, and retailers.

Challenges in Poultry Farming

Farmers often face rising feed prices, limited veterinary support, and fluctuating market demand. Our solutions address these challenges through training, technology integration, and access to reliable data.

Our Support to Farmers

Grefana Ltd partners with smallholder and commercial farmers to provide training, financial linkages, and innovative production technologies. From broiler production to layer farming, we ensure sustainable and profitable growth.
