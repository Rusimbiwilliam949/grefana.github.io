About Grefana Ltd

Innovating Agribusiness for Sustainable Livestock and Crop Production

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide farmers and agripreneurs with innovative, technology-driven solutions that improve productivity, sustainability, and profitability in livestock and crop production.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading agribusiness solutions provider, transforming farming communities and supporting sustainable food production.

Our Core Values

Innovation

We embrace new technologies and practices to optimize farm production and efficiency.

Sustainability

We implement eco-friendly practices to preserve resources for future generations.

Partnership

We collaborate closely with farmers, agripreneurs, and organizations for mutual growth.

Excellence

We maintain high standards in all our solutions to ensure measurable impact.

Our Story

Since our founding in 2015, Grefana Ltd has been committed to transforming agribusiness across Africa. From small beginnings, we now serve hundreds of farms, support over 20,000 livestock, and train thousands of farmers in modern livestock and crop practices.

2015: Company Founded
2017: 100 Farms Served
2020: Expanded to Multiple Regions
2025: 20,000+ Livestock Supported
